Frequently Asked Questions

What is the process?

StemFreeze does not require any special preparation, but you will want to be hydrated for your blood draw. The draw is about 90 seconds, and we take approximately 5 tablespoons of blood.

How secure are your preserved stem cells?

Your stem cells are kept in a highly monitored and secured-access facility. They are also stored at an incredibly low temperature (-321 F), which haltd the aging process, and storage containers are continuously monitored. For additional security, we keep a portion of your stem cells in a separate geographic location.

What happens after my blood draw?

Your blood is shipped to our laboratory and will undergo a 2-hour stem cell isolation, cryopreservation (freezing), and quality assurance process. You will receive an email confirmation and mailed certificate to inform you that your stem cells have successfully been preserved.

How do I retrieve my stem cells for future use?

Your stem cells can be retrieved at any point by contacting: contact@silenebiotech.com, or by calling our customer service hotline at 1-833-STEM-FRZ (1-833-783-6379).

Still have questions?

We’re here to answer any other questions you may have. Schedule a time to talk or just call or text us at 1-833-STEM-FRZ (1-833-783-6379).
